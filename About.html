About Us

Our solutions are based on our core belief that the whole is greater than the sum of its parts. Teams and organizations that want to realize the true potential of the truth in the Gestalt Theory can do so, provided they get critical organizational aspects right – Strategy, Structure, and Systems – the 3S model that Daksada works on. Our capability is built around this 3S Model to deliver value to our clients by helping them develop these all-important facets. Our mission is to be the preferred partner to business leaders to getting these critical fundamentals institutionalized, in order for these businesses to achieve their true potential in terms of growth, profitability, and success. Our unique Partner’s Framework makes us one of the few business consultants in the UAE who bring multiple subject matter experts together to collaborate and work towards addressing a customer challenge or maximising an available innovation opportunity. We deliver best in class solutions by applying the wisdom of the collective to define a specifically crafted solution, and backing it with world-class execution, we offer businesses the opportunity to transform themselves and reset their trajectory onto the growth path. Our experienced team of partners cumulatively bring 150+ years of hands-on experience in their respective fields – not many other business consultants in the UAE present such a strong field of industry veterans. As senior professionals who have impacted the growth in organisations across industries, we understand the drivers of growth in the Middle East markets, and the cultural levers that facilitate the same. Simply put, Daksada’s purpose is to close the ‘skill’ or ‘competency’ gap in organisations. This purpose is best explained by our name itself: ’Daks’ ( /daksh/ ) means ‘skill or ability’, and ‘Da’ ( /da/ ) means ‘to give’. We work with companies who are setting the stage for their next growth leap through a transformation mindset. As business consultants in the UAE, we partner in the process of transformation by offering a choice on the form of e ngagement – as a consultant, advisor, implementation partner, or a combination thereof. Further, we offer engagements at a flat fee, time-cost basis, milestone based, result based, or a convenient meld of these. We partner with clients over the long term, staying committed till they achieve their goals, and even linking some of our fees to the benefits our clients realize.

People Practice

Our People Practice team offers a holistic or project-based solution to the HR needs of a growing company – These span the requirements from crafting a talent strategy to setting up the HR practice, policies and processes for the organization. We also support organizations in developing organizational competency maps, HiPo planning and development, compensation and variable payment plans etc. For SME’s we offer comprehensive outsourced HR management services and 5-Year business transformation partnerships.
